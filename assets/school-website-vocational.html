Welcome to PSDC

Philippine Skills Development Center (PSDC) is a TESDA-accredited training institution committed to providing quality technical-vocational education and training (TVET) to Filipinos seeking employment, career advancement, or entrepreneurship opportunities.

Since 2010, we have trained over 15,000 students in various technical and vocational skills, with a 95% employment rate within 3 months of graduation. Our graduates work in leading companies across the Philippines and abroad as OFWs.

About PSDC

Our Mission

To provide accessible, quality technical-vocational training that develops skilled, competent, and globally competitive Filipino workers who contribute to national economic development and personal prosperity.

Our Vision

To be the leading TVET institution in Metro Manila, recognized for excellence in skills training, high employment rates, and graduates who excel in their chosen fields both locally and internationally.

TESDA Accreditation

PSDC is fully accredited by the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA), the Philippine government agency responsible for managing and supervising technical education and skills development in the country.

TESDA Registration No: NCR-2010-0123
Accreditation Level: Full Compliance
Valid Until: December 2026

TESDA-Certified Courses

All our courses lead to TESDA National Certification (NC II or NC III), recognized by employers nationwide and accepted internationally. Graduates are qualified to take the TESDA National Assessment exam.
