Barangay Captain Juan Santos

Profile

Position: Barangay Captain

Term: 2018 - 2025

Education: Bachelor of Public Administration, University of Manila

Message from the Captain

Mahal kong mga kababayan,

It is with great honor and humility that I serve as your Barangay Captain. My commitment is to work tirelessly for the development and welfare of our community. Together, we can build a safer, cleaner, and more progressive barangay for all.

Our administration focuses on three key areas: public safety, youth development, and livelihood programs. We believe that by empowering our residents, we create a stronger and more resilient community.

I encourage everyone to actively participate in our barangay programs and activities. Your voice matters, and together we can make San Pedro an even better place to live.

Maraming salamat sa inyong suporta!

- Captain Juan Santos

Accomplishments (2018-2025)

  • Established 24/7 CCTV monitoring system (2019)
  • Built new barangay health center (2020)
  • Launched "Kabataan para sa Kinabukasan" youth program (2021)
  • Installed solar street lights throughout the barangay (2022)
  • Created livelihood training program for women (2023)
  • Improved barangay road infrastructure (2024)

Programs & Advocacies

  1. Safe Barangay Program - Enhanced security through CCTV and neighborhood watch
  2. Green San Pedro - Environmental protection and waste management
  3. Youth Leadership Academy - Training the next generation of leaders
  4. Senior Citizens Welfare - Healthcare and social services for the elderly
  5. Livelihood Development - Skills training and microfinance support
