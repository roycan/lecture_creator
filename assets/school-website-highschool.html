Welcome to San Miguel National High School

Welcome to San Miguel National High School (SMNHS), a DepEd-recognized public secondary school committed to providing quality education to the youth of San Miguel and neighboring communities. Established in 1985, we have been shaping future leaders for nearly four decades.

As part of the K-12 Basic Education Program, we offer both Junior High School (Grades 7-10) and Senior High School (Grades 11-12) programs. Our Senior High School provides five academic and technical-vocational tracks, preparing students for college, employment, or entrepreneurship.

At SMNHS, we believe in holistic education that develops not just academic excellence, but also strong character, critical thinking, and active citizenship. Our dedicated faculty, modern facilities, and supportive community create an environment where every student can thrive.

About Our School

Vision

We dream of Filipinos who passionately love their country and whose values and competencies enable them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to building the nation. As a learner-centered public institution, we commit ourselves to provide quality education that empowers every student for lifelong learning and responsible citizenship.

Mission

To protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education where students learn in a child-friendly, gender-sensitive, safe, and motivating environment. Teachers facilitate learning and constantly nurture every learner. Every graduate is empowered to pursue their goals and contribute to nation-building.

Core Values

Academic Programs

Junior High School (Grades 7-10)

Our Junior High School program follows the DepEd K-12 curriculum, providing strong foundation in core subjects while developing critical thinking, creativity, and life skills.

Core Subjects:

  • Filipino, English, Mathematics, Science
  • Araling Panlipunan (Social Studies)
  • Technology and Livelihood Education (TLE)
  • Music, Arts, Physical Education, and Health (MAPEH)
  • Edukasyon sa Pagpapakatao (Values Education)

Senior High School (Grades 11-12)

Choose from five specialized tracks aligned with your interests and career goals. All tracks prepare students for college, technical training, employment, or entrepreneurship.

Academic Tracks

1. STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) STEM

  • Pre-Calculus, Basic Calculus, Statistics and Probability
  • General Biology, General Chemistry, General Physics
  • Ideal for: Engineering, Medicine, IT, Natural Sciences
  • Partner Universities: UP Diliman, DLSU, Ateneo, UST, Map√∫a

2. ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management) ABM

  • Business Math, Fundamentals of Accountancy
  • Business Finance, Organization and Management
  • Ideal for: Business, Accountancy, Economics, Entrepreneurship
  • Partner Universities: UP Diliman, DLSU, FEU, UST, Ateneo

3. HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences) HUMSS

  • Creative Writing, Philippine Politics and Governance
  • World Religions, Introduction to Philosophy
  • Ideal for: Education, Law, Mass Communication, Political Science
  • Partner Universities: UP Diliman, UST, Ateneo, PUP, PLM

4. GAS (General Academic Strand) GAS

  • Flexible curriculum, explore different fields
  • Combination of subjects from all tracks
  • Ideal for: Undecided students, multiple interests
  • Can pursue any college degree

Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Track

5. TVL - ICT (Information and Communications Technology) TVL

  • Computer Programming, Web Development

  • Ideal for: IT careers, web development, tech support
  • Certifications: TESDA NC II upon completion
