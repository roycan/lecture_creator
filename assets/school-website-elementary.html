Our Mission

To provide quality elementary education that develops well-rounded, happy, and confident learners who are ready for the future.

Our Vision

To be the leading elementary school in the community, known for academic excellence, strong values, and joyful learning experiences.

Our Core Values

  • Respect - We treat everyone with kindness and consideration
  • Excellence - We always do our best in everything we do
  • Integrity - We are honest and do what is right
  • Creativity - We think imaginatively and express ourselves
  • Responsibility - We take care of ourselves, others, and our environment

  • Library with thousands of children's books

  • Arts and crafts room

  • Cafeteria serving healthy and delicious meals

üé® Our Programs

We offer a complete elementary education from Kindergarten to Grade 6:

Kindergarten (5-6 years old)

  • Play-based learning approach
  • Basic literacy and numeracy
  • Social and emotional development
  • Arts, music, and movement
  • Mother Tongue-Based Multilingual Education (MTB-MLE)

Grade 1-3 (Primary Level)

  • Foundation skills in reading, writing, and mathematics
  • Science exploration and discovery
  • Filipino and English language development
  • Araling Panlipunan (Social Studies)
  • MAPEH (Music, Arts, Physical Education, Health)
  • Values Education

Grade 4-6 (Intermediate Level)

  • Advanced academic subjects
  • Critical thinking and problem-solving
  • Project-based learning
  • Computer literacy and basic coding
  • Leadership development
  • Preparation for junior high school

Special Programs

  • Reading Stars Program - Developing strong readers
  • Math Champions - Making math fun and easy
  • Little Scientists - Hands-on science experiments
  • Arts and Crafts Club - Unleashing creativity
  • Sports Development - Swimming, basketball, and more
  • Student Government - Leadership training for kids
