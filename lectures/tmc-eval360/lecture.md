# Pillar 3: Psychological Safety & Inclusive Culture

Built an environment where "team members" felt safe to fail and were supported individually. In leadership, this leads to higher retention and loyalty.

- The Data: Classroom Management scored *4.92* and Student Involvement scored *4.89*

-The Quote: *"Willing to help you through mistakes"* and *"Makes sure that no one is left behind"*.


# Pillar 4: The Leader’s Growth Mindset (Accountability)

Authentic leaders are transparent about their growth areas. By addressing student suggestions, you demonstrate that you are a leader who listens and iterates.

- The Data: While the "Overall Rate" is a near-perfect *4.91*, you identify specific "Operational Enhancements".

- The Quote: *"It would be nice to have a summary after every demo... other than that, none!"*.
