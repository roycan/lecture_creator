KEY SEMANTIC HTML5 ELEMENTS:
─────────────────────────────
<header>  - Top section (logo, nav, title)
<nav>     - Navigation links
<main>    - Primary content (only ONE per page)
<section> - Thematic grouping of content
<article> - Self-contained content (blog post, news)
<aside>   - Sidebar, related content
<footer>  - Bottom section (copyright, contact)

WHY USE SEMANTIC HTML?
──────────────────────
✓ Better accessibility (screen readers)
✓ Improved SEO (search engines understand structure)
✓ Easier maintenance (clear content organization)
✓ Meaningful structure (not just <div> soup)
